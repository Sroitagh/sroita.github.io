FARMEYE

This project was built during my participation in my first-ever hackathon, the "Utkranti Hackathon," hosted by my college.

The highlight of this project is the ML-based Crop Recommendation System, which utilizes an augmented dataset of 87K RGB images of healthy and diseased crop leaves, meticulously categorized into 38 classes.

Its advanced features include Profit Prediction, enabling farmers to estimate potential earnings, Crop Prediction for suggesting suitable crops based on environmental factors, and Disease Prediction to accurately identify crop diseases for timely interventions.

By harnessing the power of machine learning and integrating various technologies, FARMEYE optimizes agricultural decision-making, maximizes profitability, and promotes sustainable farming practices.
