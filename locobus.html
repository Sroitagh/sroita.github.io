LocoBus

LocoBus is an innovative University Bus Tracking app designed to revolutionize transportation services on campus.

With its real-time tracking and monitoring capabilities, the app aims to enhance the efficiency and convenience of commuting for students, faculty, and staff.

By providing up-to-date bus location and arrival time information, LocoBus enables users to plan their journeys conveniently, minimizing wait times and reducing uncertainty.

The app addresses common challenges in college transportation, such as unreliable bus schedules and overcrowding, by offering accurate and reliable tracking features.

LocoBus prioritizes user safety by implementing security measures like emergency alerts and secure data handling.

Overall, LocoBus aims to provide a seamless and efficient bus tracking experience, enhancing transportation convenience and contributing to a more connected and optimized campus environment.

Technologies:
  • - Flutter
  • - Node Js
  • - Firebase
  • - GoogleCloud
  • - GoogleMapsAPIs
